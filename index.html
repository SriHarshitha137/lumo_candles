Our story

Slow craft, deep intention

Every candle carries the memory of the hands that made it.

Lumo Candles began with a simple wish: to make home feel calmer, more personal, and beautifully lived in. Each vessel is poured by hand in small batches, allowing the wax, wick, and fragrance to settle with care.

We work with natural soy wax, cotton core wicks, and refined scent blends that unfold slowly. The result is a candle that feels considered from first light to final glow.
